About Journal Automation

This platform was built to simplify the complex workflows involved in managing research journals. By integrating AI models like Gemini and Groq, along with CORE’s Research API, the system automates tasks such as:

  • ✔ Collecting and storing journal input data
  • ✔ Automatically generating structured summaries, references, and insights
  • ✔ Searching related articles using CORE
  • ✔ Producing professional outputs in both HTML and PDF

Our goal is to empower editors, reviewers, and researchers by reducing manual work and improving the quality of knowledge dissemination.
